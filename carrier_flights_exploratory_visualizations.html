Conclusion¶

When considering what factors contribute most to whether or not a flight will be delayed, the most important thing to factor is which airline carrier you will be using. Some carriers have great records for getting passengers to their destinations on time, while others have a record of increased cancelled, delayed, or diverted flights. Airtime and distance seem to not affect the proportion of flights that are Cancelled, Diverted, or Delayed.
